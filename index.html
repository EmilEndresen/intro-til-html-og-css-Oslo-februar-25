Shoebill Stork

The name shoebill comes from the shape of their bill, which looks a bit like a Dutch clog shoe.
But taxonomists who described it also thought the bill shape resembled the head of a baleen whale, and named the species Balaeniceps rex—which means “whale-head king.” Other informal names for these birds are the boat bill, the bog bird, and the swamp king.
